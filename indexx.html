Who We Are

Dobson Avenue is the general partner for the GrowChicago Fund.

Dobson Avenue’s mission is accelerating the growth of up-and-coming companies and reshaping
the future of food while being committed to investing in minority, women, LGBTQIA+,
immigrant and veteran owned food businesses throughout Chicagoland.
